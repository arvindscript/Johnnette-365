Tips to check adulteration in raw black peppercorn, kali mirch powder

By: Lifestyle Desk New Delhi | February 18, 2025 09:30 IST Newsguard clock_logo4 min read facebook twitter whatsapp Reddit black peppercorns Here's how to check for adulteration (Photo: Freepik) Black peppercorn is one of the most widely used spices around the globe. With a pungent heat mixed with earthy and woody notes, black peppercorn lends flavour and makes a dish nutritious. But in recent times, black pepper has also been plagued by food adulteration, say experts. “Many cheap products and chemicals are mixed with black peppercorn to increase profit. Many clinical studies have discovered various types of chemicals or other products mixed in pepper powder and peppercorns,” said Rupeet Kaur, nutritionist and founder and CEO, Diet Ryt. Some of these chemicals/adulterants are so toxic that they may lead to heart failure, kidney dysfunction, liver disorders, stressed Kaur. Following are adulterants that are mixed Papaya seeds Papaya seeds look similar to black peppercorns after drying. “They are almost similar in size and colour.
